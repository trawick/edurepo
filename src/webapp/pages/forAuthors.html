Curriculum designers:

  • The formal curriculum description moves closer to the forefront of the conversation around individual classes and educational materials because the individual learning objectives in the formal curriculum description are the key to communication of activities between teachers and parents.
  • The formal description, "I Can" statements, and informal descriptions of individual objectives will be viewed by all involved parties via the central use of the objectives.
  • From the curriculum designers we need an outline of each course with different types of information around each objective.

Creators of educational materials:

  • Educators who contribute material to the repository will be able to reuse it in a variety of software.
  • The material must be freely available under common terms, similar to material in Wikipedia.
  • Not all materials are of a form suitable for inclusion in the repository, but the index to outside resources can be used to relate the materials to specific objectives.
