Curriculum designers,

  • The curriculum definitions you normally work with will be tagged to identify the individual types of information, after which they can be imported into the system.
  • The formal curriculum description will move closer to the forefront of the conversation around individual classes and educational materials because the individual learning objectives in the formal curriculum description are the key to communication of activities between teachers and parents.
  • The formal description, "I Can" statements, and informal descriptions of individual objectives will be easily accessed by teachers, parents, and students by all via the central focus on objectives.

Creators of educational materials,

  • Educators who contribute material to the repository will be able to reuse it in a variety of software.
  • The material must be freely available under common terms, similar to material in Wikipedia.
  • Not all materials are of a form suitable for inclusion in the repository, but an index to outside resources can be used to relate the materials to specific objectives.
