In short:

  • The repository is the means to share data and make it machine readable, enhancing most educational software by virtue of its access to more materials and, just as importantly, to access those materials most pertinent to the student's current needs.
  • Specialty software which currently requires teachers to rekey or otherwise manipulate the educational materials can be much easier for users when they can reuse material from the repository.
  • Ready access to data should incentivize experiments in new methods or situations for learning.
