Teachers,

In order for you and your student families to make best use of the system, we need you to maintain a calendar showing the current learning objective(s) for your classes which participate in the project. Easy to use software will make this quick, and in a short time you can lay out projected progress over the coming days or weeks and easily adjust it later.

You and your student families will then be able to use different software to access materials relevant to the current learning objectives.

See the demo on the parents page.

If you are teaching a course which is not yet defined in the system, you and your colleagues can collaborate on defining it.

You are also authors of materials.
